    Intro

    Malaysia is a Southeast Asia country which consists of Peninsular Malaysia and Malaysian Borneo.

    The capital city is Kuala Lumpur.

    The country is multi-ethnic and multi cultural with 3 major ethnic groups which are Malay, Chinese and Indian, along with other indigenous such as Iban, Kadazan and Dusun.
